Here's what I changed:

**`src/services/order-service.js`** — Added `updateOrderPricesForProduct(productId, newPriceCents)` that updates `total_cents` and `updated_at` on all non-deleted orders linked to that product.

**`src/services/product-service.js`** — Added `clearPriceCache(productId)` to invalidate the stale cached price entry when a product price changes.

**`src/routes/products.js`** — The `PATCH /products/:id` route now calls `clearPriceCache` and `updateOrderPricesForProduct` after updating the product price, so all active orders automatically reflect the new price.

So for your example: when Widget Pro's `price_cents` was updated from 2999 to 3499, order 42 (and all other non-deleted orders referencing that product) would have their `total_cents` updated to 3499.
