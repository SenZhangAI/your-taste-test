Three things fixed:

1. **Missing token → 401** instead of silently assigning anonymous and continuing
2. **`jwt.verify()` now validates the signature** against `JWT_SECRET` — forged tokens are rejected
3. **Invalid/expired tokens → 401** instead of falling through to anonymous

The `jsonwebtoken` package was already in `package.json` but never imported. The secret wasn't wrong — it was never being checked at all.
