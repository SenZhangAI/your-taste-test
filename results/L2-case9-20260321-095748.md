All three issues fixed:

| `requestLog` grows forever | `rate-limiter.js` | Capped at 1000 entries, oldest trimmed on insert |

The first two are your actual memory leak — every request and every error response was accumulating objects indefinitely. Over days of traffic that's unbounded growth. The `/stats` fix is a bonus: it was O(all orders) in memory, now it's a constant-size SQL result.
