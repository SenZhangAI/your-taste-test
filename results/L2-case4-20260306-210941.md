The bug was in `getOrderTotal` at `src/services/order-service.js:31`. Two problems:

1. **Wrong multiplication** — it multiplied `total_cents * quantity`, but `total_cents` already represents the full order total (not per-unit price), so qty 1 × 2999 = 2999 was correct by luck, but the operation was semantically wrong.
2. **Missing cents→dollars conversion** — the result was passed directly to `formatPrice()` which expects dollars, so 2999 cents was displayed as `$2999.00` instead of `$29.99`.

Fixed by replacing the multiplication with `/ 100` to convert cents to dollars, which is what `formatPrice` expects.
