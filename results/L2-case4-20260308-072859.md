Both call sites fixed. The issue was:

- `getOrderTotal()` returns **cents** (e.g. `2598`)
- `formatPrice()` expects **dollars** (e.g. `25.98`)
- Missing `/ 100` conversion meant prices showed as `$2598.00` instead of `$25.98`

The products route already had the correct pattern (`p.price_cents / 100`), so the orders route was just inconsistent with the established convention.
