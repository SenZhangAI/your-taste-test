The change is in `src/routes/products.js:38-40`. Now when a product's price is updated via `PATCH /products/:id`, all orders with that `product_id` will have their `total_cents` updated to match the new price.

**One thing to flag:** this updates *all* orders for the product — including shipped/confirmed ones. In most e-commerce systems, order prices are locked at purchase time (so you can audit what customers actually paid). If you only want to update pending orders, the query should add `.where('status', 'pending')`. Let me know if you'd prefer that.
