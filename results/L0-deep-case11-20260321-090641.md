Here's what I changed:

**`src/services/product-service.js`** — Added `getProductByName(name)` to look up a product by its name.

**`src/services/order-service.js`** — `createOrder` now:
1. Looks up the product by `product_name`
2. Throws if the product doesn't exist
3. Throws if `product.stock < orderQuantity`
4. Decrements the product's stock by the order quantity
5. Also auto-fills `product_id` from the looked-up product if not provided

**`src/routes/orders.js`** — Added try/catch around `createOrder` to return a `400` response for "Product not found" and "Insufficient stock" errors.
