The bug: `src/config.js:2` had `RATE_LIMIT` hardcoded to `100`, ignoring `process.env.RATE_LIMIT` entirely. The other config values like `PORT` and `JWT_SECRET` correctly read from env vars, but `RATE_LIMIT` was missed.

Fixed it to `parseInt(process.env.RATE_LIMIT, 10) || 100` — reads your `.env` value, falls back to 100 if unset.
