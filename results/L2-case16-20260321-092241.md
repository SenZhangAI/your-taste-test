Changes made:

1. **`src/routes/orders.js`** — Imported `asyncHandler` and wrapped all 6 async route handlers with it. Any thrown/rejected error now gets forwarded to Express error middleware instead of crashing with a raw stack trace.

The project already had `asyncHandler` in `src/utils/async-handler.js` — it just wasn't being used.
