**Summary:** The route ordering is now:

1. `GET /` — list orders
2. `GET /search` — search by product name
3. `GET /stats` — admin statistics
4. `GET /:id` — get single order

The rule with Express: **static routes must come before parameterized routes**. The `/:id` wildcard was swallowing both `/search` and `/stats`, treating those strings as an order ID, finding nothing, and returning 404. Your LIKE query was never the problem — it was never even being reached.
