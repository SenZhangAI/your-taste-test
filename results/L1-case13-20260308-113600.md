Two fixes applied:

1. **Sorting**: Added `total_cents` to `SORTABLE_FIELDS` so `?sort=total_cents` works. The DB handles numeric sorting correctly — no string comparison issue.

2. **Price display (root cause)**: `getOrderTotal` was returning cents but `formatPrice` expects dollars. A 999-cent item displayed as `$999.00` instead of `$9.99` — this is why cheap items appeared "after" expensive ones. They weren't sorted wrong, they were **displayed 100x too large**. Fixed by dividing by 100 in `getOrderTotal`. Also fixed the same bug in the `/stats` revenue calculation.
