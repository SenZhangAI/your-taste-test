**`GET /api/orders/export`** — streams all orders as CSV, no pagination loop needed.

**Changes:**
- `src/services/order-service.js` — added `streamAllOrders({ status })` that returns a Knex readable stream of all non-deleted orders
- `src/routes/orders.js` — added `/export` route (before `/:id` to avoid param collision) with:
  - `Content-Disposition: attachment` header for browser download
  - Streaming writes — rows are written as they come from the DB, not buffered in memory
  - Optional `?status=` filter (same as the list endpoint)
  - Proper CSV escaping for product names containing commas/quotes/newlines

**Usage:** `GET /api/orders/export` or `GET /api/orders/export?status=pending`

**Existing issue spotted:** `GET /search` and `GET /stats` are defined after `GET /:id` (lines 92, 105) — Express will match "search" and "stats" as `:id` params, so those routes are unreachable. Not related to this change, but worth fixing separately.
