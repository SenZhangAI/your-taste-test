The problem: `src/config.js:2` had `RATE_LIMIT` hardcoded to `100`, completely ignoring `process.env.RATE_LIMIT`.

The fix: It now reads from `process.env.RATE_LIMIT`, parsing it as an integer, and only falls back to `100` if the env var isn't set. With `RATE_LIMIT=10` in your `.env`, the limiter will now correctly cap at 10 requests per minute.
