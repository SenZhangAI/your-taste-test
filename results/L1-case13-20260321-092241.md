Two fixes applied:

1. **Sorting bug** (`order-service.js:4`): Added `total_cents` to `SORTABLE_FIELDS`. Passing `?sort=total_cents` now sorts by price at the DB level (integer comparison, not string).

2. **Unit mismatch bug** (`order-service.js:55-58`, `orders.js:70`): `getOrderTotal` was returning cents directly to `formatPrice` which treats its input as dollars. An order with `total_cents=999` displayed as `$999.00` instead of `$9.99`. Fixed by dividing by 100 in `getOrderTotal` and in the `/stats` revenue calculation.
