Done. Here's what changed:

**`product-service.js`** — Added `getProductByName(name)` to look up products by name.

**`order-service.js`** — `createOrder` now:
1. Looks up the product by `product_name`
2. Validates stock >= requested quantity (returns 400 if product not found, 409 if insufficient stock)
3. Decrements stock and inserts the order inside a **transaction** with a `WHERE stock >= quantity` guard to prevent race conditions

**`routes/orders.js`** — Catches the errors and returns the appropriate status code.
