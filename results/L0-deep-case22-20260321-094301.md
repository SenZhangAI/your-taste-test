The problem was **route ordering**, not the LIKE query. Express matches routes in definition order, and `/:id` was defined before `/search` and `/stats`. When you hit `/api/orders/search?q=Widget`, Express matched `/:id` with `id = "search"`, found no order with that ID, and returned 404.

**Fix:** Moved `/search` and `/stats` (static path segments) **above** `/:id` (parameterized). The general rule: always define specific/static routes before wildcard/parameterized ones.
