Summary of what was leaking and what changed:

| Source | Problem | Fix |
|--------|---------|-----|
| `rate-limiter.js` `requestLog` | Every request appended forever | Capped at 1000 entries |
| `logger.js` `recentErrors` | Every error appended forever | Capped at 50 entries |
| `orders.js` `/stats` | Loads all orders into JS to count | Aggregates in SQL |

The `requestLog` was almost certainly the dominant leak — it grows with every single request and stores objects with strings. At a few hundred req/s that's GBs within days.
