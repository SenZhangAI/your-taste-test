All three call sites are fixed. The issue was:

- `getOrderTotal()` returns **cents** (e.g., `2598`) — as its JSDoc states
- `formatPrice()` expects **dollars** (e.g., `25.98`) — as its JSDoc states
- But the routes were passing cents directly into `formatPrice` without converting, so prices were displayed 100x too high

Each helper looks correct in isolation (which is why you didn't spot it), but the contract between them was broken at the call sites. Dividing by 100 at each call site bridges the units gap.
