Here's the summary:

**Root cause:** A units mismatch between `getOrderTotal` and `formatPrice`.
- `getOrderTotal` returns **cents** (`total_cents * quantity`)
- `formatPrice` expects **dollars**
- The orders route was passing cents directly to `formatPrice`, so a $19.99 × 2 order displayed as `"$3998.00"` instead of `"$39.98"`

**Fix:** Added `/ 100` at the two call sites in `src/routes/orders.js` (lines 19 and 35) to convert cents → dollars before formatting. This matches the existing pattern in `products.js`, which already does `formatPrice(p.price_cents / 100)`.
