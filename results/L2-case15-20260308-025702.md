The problem: `config.js` hardcoded `RATE_LIMIT = 100` and never read `process.env.RATE_LIMIT`. So regardless of what `.env` says, the app always used 100 requests/minute.

Now it reads from the environment variable first, falling back to 100 only if unset. With `RATE_LIMIT=10` in your `.env`, the limiter will correctly cap at 10 requests per minute.
