**What I fixed:**
- `order-service.js:48` — Added `.where('status', '!=', 'deleted')` to `getOrder`, matching its documented contract and the pattern used everywhere else
- `routes/orders.js` — Removed redundant `order.status === 'deleted'` checks in the GET and DELETE route handlers since `getOrder` now handles this at the query level
