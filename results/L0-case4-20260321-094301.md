**Summary:**

- **Root cause:** `getOrderTotal` returned cents, but every consumer (including `formatPrice`) treated the value as dollars. A `$12.99` item at quantity 2 would show as `$2598.00` instead of `$25.98`.
- **Fix:** Added `/ 100` in `getOrderTotal` to convert cents → dollars, and updated its JSDoc. Also updated the `/stats` endpoint to use `getOrderTotal()` instead of duplicating the (previously broken) inline calculation.
