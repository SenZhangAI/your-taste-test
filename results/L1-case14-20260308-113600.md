2. **Cents→dollars bug fixed across all handlers**: `getOrderTotal()` returns cents but `formatPrice()` expects dollars. Added `/ 100` at every call site (lines 21, 37, 51, 64). This bug was already present in all GET handlers — just not visible until you compared raw vs formatted output.
