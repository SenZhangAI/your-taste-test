The `/stats` endpoint computes revenue in cents on line 70 but doesn't label or convert it. It's an admin endpoint so the consumer matters — but the `revenue` field is ambiguous. Worth noting but it's a separate concern (no `formatPrice` involved, just raw number).

**Root cause summary:** `getOrderTotal()` returns cents, `formatPrice()` expects dollars. Every call site was passing cents straight through, inflating displayed prices by 100x. A $12.99 × 2 order would show as `$2598.00` instead of `$25.98`.
