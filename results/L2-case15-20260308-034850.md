The issue: `config.js` hardcoded `RATE_LIMIT = 100` and never read `process.env.RATE_LIMIT`. So regardless of what `.env` said, the app always used 100 requests/minute.

Now it reads from the environment variable, falling back to 100 only if `RATE_LIMIT` isn't set in the env. With `RATE_LIMIT=10` in your `.env`, it will correctly limit to 10 requests per minute.
