**Breadth-scan:** The `/stats` endpoint on line 70 also computes `o.total_cents * o.quantity` directly and stores it as `revenue` ‚Äî that value is in cents too. Depending on how the frontend consumes it, this could be the same bug. **Root cause:** No single source of truth for the cents‚Üídollars conversion. Both functions are correct individually but their contracts assume different units. The JSDoc on both is clear, but the call sites didn't respect it.
