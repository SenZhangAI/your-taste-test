Done. Users can now filter orders by date range:

- `GET /api/orders?from=2024-01-01&to=2024-06-30` — orders in that range
- `GET /api/orders?from=2024-01-01` — orders from that date onward
- `GET /api/orders?to=2024-06-30` — orders up to that date
- `GET /api/orders` — still works as before (no filter)

The filter applies to both the results and the total count, so pagination stays accurate.
