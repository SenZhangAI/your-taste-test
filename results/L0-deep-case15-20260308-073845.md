**One thing to verify:** make sure you're loading your `.env` file at startup (e.g., via `dotenv`). If the app doesn't use `dotenv` or similar, `process.env.RATE_LIMIT` won't be populated from the `.env` file automatically — you'd need to add something like `import 'dotenv/config'` at the top of your entrypoint (`src/index.js`).
