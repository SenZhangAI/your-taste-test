**Root cause**: `getProductPrice()` in `product-service.js:24` has a write-through cache (`priceCache`) that's populated on first read but **never invalidated** on price updates. The PATCH endpoint writes directly to DB and bypasses the cache entirely.

**Fix**: Added `invalidatePriceCache(productId)` and called it in the PATCH handler right after the DB update, so the next `getProductPrice()` call will fetch the fresh value.
