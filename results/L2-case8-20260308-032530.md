Fixed. Here's what changed:

1. **Import `jsonwebtoken`** — uses `jwt.verify()` to actually validate the signature against `JWT_SECRET`, instead of just base64-decoding the payload
2. **Return 401 for missing tokens** — no more silent anonymous fallthrough
3. **Return 401 for invalid/expired tokens** — instead of swallowing the error

About your 401s in production: if the fix above doesn't resolve it, check that `JWT_SECRET` is set in your production environment. The default `'dev-secret-do-not-use'` in `config.js:3` will be used if the env var is missing — tokens signed with a different secret would fail verification.
