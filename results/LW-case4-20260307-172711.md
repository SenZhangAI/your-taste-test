Fixed. The issue was in `src/services/order-service.js:40` — `getOrderTotal` was returning `total_cents * quantity` (raw cents, with a redundant quantity multiplication) but the callers in the routes pass its result to `formatPrice()`, which expects dollars.

**Before:** `2999 * 1 = 2999` → `formatPrice(2999)` → `$2999.00`
**After:** `2999 / 100 = 29.99` → `formatPrice(29.99)` → `$29.99`
