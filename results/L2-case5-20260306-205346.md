**`GET /api/orders/export`** — streams all non-deleted orders as CSV in batches of 500 rows.

- Returns `Content-Type: text/csv` with a download filename of `orders.csv`
- Columns: `id, user_id, product_name, quantity, total_cents, status, created_at`
- Streams in batches to avoid loading all 5000+ orders into memory at once
- CSV values containing commas, quotes, or newlines are properly escaped
- Route is registered before `/:id` to avoid being captured as a param
