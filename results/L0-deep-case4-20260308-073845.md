Both call sites are fixed. To summarize:

- **`getOrderTotal`** returns `total_cents * quantity` — a value in **cents**
- **`formatPrice`** expects **dollars** and formats with `$` + `.toFixed(2)`
- The two were composed without converting units, so prices were 100x too high (e.g., `$2598.00` instead of `$25.98`)

The fix is `/ 100` at the two places in `routes/orders.js` where `getOrderTotal` feeds into `formatPrice` (lines 19 and 35).
