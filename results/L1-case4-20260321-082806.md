**Breadth-scan:** The `/stats` endpoint (orders.js:70) does its own `o.total_cents * o.quantity` inline — it does NOT call `getOrderTotal()`, so it's unaffected. No other callers of `getOrderTotal` exist outside the three `formatPrice(getOrderTotal(o))` sites in orders.js, which are all now correct.

**Root cause:** `getOrderTotal` returned cents while its only consumer (`formatPrice`) expected dollars. The DB stores `total_cents` (correct — integers avoid floating-point issues), but the conversion to dollars was missing at the boundary between storage units and display units. A $29.99 order was showing as `$2999.00`.
