Fixed. Here's what changed in `src/middleware/auth.js`:

1. **Requests without a token are now rejected** with `401 Missing or malformed Authorization header` instead of being silently allowed through as "anonymous".

2. **Tokens are now cryptographically verified** using `jwt.verify(token, JWT_SECRET)` instead of just base64-decoding the payload. This means forged/tampered tokens will be rejected with `401 Invalid or expired token`.

3. **Expired tokens are also rejected** — `jwt.verify` checks the `exp` claim automatically.

Public paths (`/health`, `/api/products`) are still exempted as before.
